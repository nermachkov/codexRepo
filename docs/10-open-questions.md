# Open Questions

## Product Questions

- What genre are we building?
- Is it a game, app, tool, or hybrid interactive experience?
- What is the one-sentence pitch?
- Who is the target player?
- Should the tone be cozy, competitive, funny, serious, or cinematic?
- Should sessions last 30 seconds, 3 minutes, or 15 minutes?

## Game Design Questions

- What does the player do every few seconds?
- What is the main challenge?
- What is the reward?
- What makes the player improve?
- What ends a session?
- What brings the player back tomorrow?

## Business Questions

- Free, paid, ads, or in-app purchases?
- Is this a portfolio project, a commercial product, or a learning project?
- Is speed more important than polish for the first release?
- Do we want a public GitHub repository long-term?

## Technical Questions

- Phaser, PixiJS, vanilla TypeScript, or another stack?
- TWA or Capacitor for Android?
- Online-only or offline-first?
- Do we need a backend?
- Do we need analytics?

## Google Play Questions

- What developer name will appear on the store listing?
- What support email should be used?
- Where will the privacy policy be hosted?
- What countries should be targeted first?
- What age rating is intended?

## Decisions Needed Next

1. Pick the product genre.
2. Pick a working title.
3. Pick the technical stack.
4. Pick the Android packaging path.
5. Decide whether the first release collects any data.
