# Monetization

## Initial Recommendation

Do not monetize the first prototype. Validate the coloring feel, content pipeline, retention, and Google Play packaging first.

## Possible Models

### Free With No Ads

Best for early release and testing.

Pros:

- Lowest compliance complexity.
- Best user trust.
- Faster review path.

Cons:

- No revenue.

### Paid App

Pros:

- Simple UX.
- No ad SDK.
- No in-app purchase economy.

Cons:

- Harder acquisition.
- Users expect high polish upfront.

### Cosmetic In-App Purchases

Pros:

- Fair if gameplay is not paywalled.
- Can support long-term development.

Cons:

- Requires Google Play Billing.
- Requires entitlement logic.
- More testing and support.

### Premium Artwork Packs

Pros:

- Natural fit for coloring apps.
- Easy to explain to users.
- Does not need to affect the core coloring mechanic.

Cons:

- Requires enough free content to feel fair.
- Requires Google Play Billing.
- Requires a content entitlement system.

### Remove Ads Subscription

Pros:

- Common in the category.
- Can pair with hints or premium packs.

Cons:

- Ads should not be added only to make removal valuable.
- Subscription pressure can hurt the calm product feeling.

### Ads

Pros:

- Common for casual mobile games.

Cons:

- SDK privacy complexity.
- UX risk.
- Extra Data Safety declarations.
- Potential child-directed content considerations.

## Recommendation By Phase

MVP:

- No ads.
- No purchases.
- Bundled free starter artwork.

Beta:

- Consider optional cosmetics only if retention is promising.
- Consider premium packs only after the content pipeline is reliable.

Production:

- Choose one monetization model, not several at once.

## Purchase Design Rules

- No pay-to-win for competitive modes.
- No dark patterns.
- No accidental purchase traps.
- Clear restore purchases flow.
- Purchases must survive reinstall if they are durable entitlements.

## Store Listing Impact

If monetization is added, update:

- Store listing.
- Privacy policy.
- Data Safety form.
- App content declarations.
- QA checklist.
